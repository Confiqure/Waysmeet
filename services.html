Flames of Hope

Flames of Hope is program we offer that acknowledges people's other needs in life. We have volunteers that help fill other's needs for free. A few topics that we offer assistance in are health care, car repair, and thrift shopping. We also sign petitions, help pledge support, and contribute money or other resources to help reunite kids with their families. Sometimes, we Outreach to the Forest Park and Babcock Communities on the UNH campus. To get involved in this program, stop by our house on Mill Road!


Cornucopia Food Pantry

Our biggest service we offer is Cornucopia Food Pantry. Our Food Pantry offers food on a weekly basis to those in need. We also provide food baskets three times a year. We currently serve about 1,500 customers a year which feeds about 6,000 people. For more information on receiving food or helping out, visit here.

Hours of Operation:
Summer (Jun-Aug): Tuesdays, 4:00-5:30 PM
Academic Year (Sept-May): Tuesdays, 4:00-5:30 PM, and Fridays, 12:00-2:00PM


Mindfulness Meditation

One of our niche services that we offer is a variation of relaxation, mindfulness mediation. Some benefits of this is better memory, greater self-awareness, and improved learning. We have many repeat customers that come weekly because of the benefits that this meditation reaps. We welcome everyone here regardless of race, religion, and cultures you are. Meditation is always beneficial to your mental health, making your life more clear and giving you a positive spirit. Give us a call or stop by to learn more about how to get involved!


Drum Circles & Community Dinners

Another service we offer is group drum circles and community dinners. Our drum circles are a celebration of life, community, and music-making in the moment. Our circles are run by our very own volunteers. Some benefits our customers have from coming to these are accelerated physcial healing and boosting of their immune system. People have also told us that they have reduced stress, anxiety, and tension after attending our sessions. Besides group drum circles, we also offer community dinners. These help us reach out to the community to connect and get feedback from everyone. Reach out to us if you want to get involved with dinner planning or if you want to attend. We hope to see you all there!
