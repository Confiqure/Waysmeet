Cornucopia Food Pantry

Our biggest service we offer is Cornucopia Food Pantry. Our Food Pantry offers food on a weekly basis to those in need. We also provide food baskets three times a year. We currently serve about 1,500 customers a year which feeds about 6,000 people. For more information on receiving food or helping out, visit here
